The **Profile** section is where you manage your user account. Access it from the **main menu** in the top bar.

## Account settings

- **First name** and **Last name** — your display name across Tweenly.
- **Email** — the email address linked to your account (used for login).

## License & Billing

View your current license plan and upgrade from the **License & Billing** tab.

For upgrade steps, plan details, and payment information, see [Manage License](/getting-started/manage-license/).

## Password

Change your password from the Profile section. Enter your current password and the new password to confirm the change.

## Delete account

You can permanently delete your account from the Profile section.

:::danger
Deleting your account removes all your data and cannot be undone.
:::
